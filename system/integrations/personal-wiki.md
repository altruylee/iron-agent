# Personal Wiki Integration

The personal wiki is the durable knowledge layer of Iron Agent.

## Directory

- [Principle](#principle)
- [Required Reads](#required-reads)
- [Read Path From Here](#read-path-from-here)

## Principle

- Preserve raw material before summarizing.
- Use `sources/` for single-source summaries.
- Use `entities/` for concrete objects.
- Use `concepts/` for reusable ideas.
- Use `explorations/` for cross-source judgments.
- Keep task outputs in `output/` unless they become durable knowledge.

## Required Reads

Before writing wiki pages, read:

1. `workspace/workspace-config.md`
2. `wiki/_schema.md`

## Read Path From Here

| Need | Next location |
|---|---|

| Concept page | `wiki/concepts/` |
